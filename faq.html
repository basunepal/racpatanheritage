FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Rotaract Club of Patan Heritage?

You can join us by filling out our membership form (available online or in person), attending a few of our meetings or events, and expressing your interest to our Membership Committee. We welcome young, motivated individuals who are passionate about service, leadership, and personal growth.

When was Patan Heritage chartered, and what does Charter Day mean?

Our club was officially chartered on 20th October 2022.
"Charter Day" marks the formal recognition of a Rotaract club by Rotary International. It is the day our club officially became a part of the global Rotary/Rotaract family, empowering us to serve our community under the Rotary banner.

Is there a membership fee to join?

Yes, there is a yearly membership fee that every member is required to pay. This fee contributes to Rotary International and Rotaract District 3292 for operational, administrative, and training purposes. The exact amount is shared with prospective members during the orientation.

When and where do you meet?

We hold our regular meetings every Saturday at 10:00 AM at Relex IT Solution, Chyasal, Lalitpur. Members are notified in advance about any changes or special meeting locations.

What are the advantages of joining the Rotaract Club?

Joining Rotaract offers you:

  • Leadership and personal development opportunities
  • A strong local and global network of young professionals
  • Involvement in impactful community service projects
  • International exposure through conferences and exchange programs
  • Friendship, fun, and lifelong learning
It's a platform to grow while giving back to society.

Is Rotaract only for students?

Not at all. Rotaract is open to anyone aged 18 to 30+, whether you are a student, working professional, or entrepreneur. Our club values diversity and encourages people from all walks of life to contribute and lead.

Can I attend a meeting before becoming a member?

Yes, prospective members are encouraged to attend a few meetings to understand our culture, activities, and values. This also helps both you and the club assess mutual fit before proceeding with the formal onboarding process.
